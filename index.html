01

Ongoing bookkeeping in QuickBooks Online

Your books, kept current and accurate month after month, so you always know where you stand.

  • QuickBooks Online (QBO) software subscription
  • Every bank transaction categorized
  • Income & expenses recorded with receipts attached
  • Bank, credit card & loan reconciliations
  • Balance Sheet, P&L, and Cash Flow reports

QuickBooks Online set up

Starting fresh, or migrating from another platform? We get QBO configured correctly from day one.

About

Hi, I'm Jamie.

I started this business for some of the same reasons you started yours — I wanted more freedom and flexibility, and I wanted to use my skills to bring real value to some of the hardest workers out there: small business owners.

I'm a Louisiana native, and I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Accounting from LSU. Geaux Tigers! I'm a Certified Public Accountant, a Certified Internal Auditor, and a Certified QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor. My family recently relocated back to Louisiana from Northwest Arkansas, and we're happy to be back home — but because we're a virtual firm, we happily accept clients from anywhere in the US.

My knack for numbers and the attention to detail I honed as an auditor and business-controls advisor mean you can rest easy that every i is dotted and t is crossed — with meaningful financial reports that help you make informed decisions.
